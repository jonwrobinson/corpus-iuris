* Caveat lector:

  The following list is added to incremetally and *not* systematically
  (except for books 1 and 2). An ideal entry, and there are none yet,
  will list its incipit, where an *authentica* can be found in the
  *Code*, provide its rubric, and give a cross-reference to its
  location in the *Authenticum* as well as to the *Novels*.

  Needless to say, everything listed so far would benefit from some
  cross-checking.
